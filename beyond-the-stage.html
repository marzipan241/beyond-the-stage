Data Description:

  • Data included thus far represents the fundamental organization of the project, by tour dates and locations for the band BTS’ world tour, “BTS World Tour: Love Yourself”.

  • “love_yourself” includes all dates, geographic locations, attendance, and revenue of each concert stop

  • Attendance and revenue for the following shows were found as the sum of all shows at the respective location: Seoul, North American cities, London, Berlin, Paris, Japan cities, Taoyuan, Hong Kong, Bangkok

  • Data for each show is the sum divided by the number of shows per city, and may differ from actual venues, consider redividing in proportion to relative stadium capacities

  • While the source is the summary chart for the Wikipedia page for “BTS World Tour: Love Yourself”, each of the numbers in the chart links to local coverage of concert sales, pulling from numerous news sources
